What happens if a bike is stolen?

We follow an established procedure for addressing any bike theft. First we rely on our bike's unique sensor and positioning system to intervene and locate the bike if a theft is in progress.

If we are unable to work with local authorities and recover the bike, we will conduct a failure analysis and determine if the bike was stolen based on user error or by criminal wit. You are responsible for properly locking a redbars bike to a proper bike lock. If it is deemed that the last user locked the bike imporperly and resulted in the theft, we will fine the user as such.
