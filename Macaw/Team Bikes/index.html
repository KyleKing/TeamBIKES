What is a bikeshare?

RedBars provides bikes all around campus that users can quickly rent, ride to their destination, and return. The next rider can pick up the a same or new bike to ride to their destination.

Welcome to the bikeshare of the future

Quickly rent any bike on campus and return to any bike rack

How do I use the RedBars bikeshare?

1. Go to https://redbarbikes.com/map and take a look at all of the available bikes. Once you have found a nearby bike, double tap it to reserve it.

2. Use your RedBars ID and tap the panel on the bike lock to unlock. When disconnected from the bike rack, push the bike lock into the riding position.

3. When at your destination, tap your ID once more to unlock the bike and lock it to the nearest bike rack.

RedBars is the fastest, most convenient, and quickest way to get across campus. Never be late to class, a date, or any meeting again.
